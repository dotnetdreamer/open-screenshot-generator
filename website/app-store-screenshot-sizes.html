Troubleshooting

Why App Store Connect rejects screenshots

  • Wrong dimensions. The upload must match an accepted size exactly, one pixel off fails. Export at the exact size instead of resizing by hand
  • Alpha channel present. A PNG with transparency is rejected even at the right size. Flatten before uploading
  • Wrong slot. A 6.9 inch image uploaded to the iPad slot fails the size check. Match each file to its device class
  • Mac aspect ratio. Mac screenshots must be 16:10, a 16:9 capture from a non-Mac display fails

Questions

Fair questions, straight answers

What size should App Store screenshots be in 2026?

For iPhone, 1290 by 2796 pixels, the 6.9 inch tier which also accepts 1260 by 2736 and 1320 by 2868. For iPad, 2064 by 2752 pixels, which also accepts 2048 by 2732. Apple scales these down to smaller devices automatically

How many screenshots can I upload?

One to 10 per device size, in PNG or JPEG, with no alpha channels or transparency

Do I need separate screenshots for every iPhone model?

No. Apple's own guidance is to provide only the highest resolution screenshots required, and they scale down to smaller device sizes automatically. One 6.9 inch set covers every modern iPhone unless you want per tier control

Do iPad screenshots have a required size?

Yes. If your app runs on iPad you must provide 13 inch screenshots at 2064 by 2752 or 2048 by 2732, portrait or landscape. Smaller iPad tiers are optional and scale from the 13 inch set

What sizes do App Store preview videos need?

App previews use different dimensions from screenshots: 886 by 1920 for modern iPhones and 1200 by 1600 for iPads, at up to 30 fps in H.264, 15 to 30 seconds long. The app preview video maker page has the full spec
