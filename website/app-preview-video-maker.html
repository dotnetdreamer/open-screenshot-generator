The rules

What App Store Connect accepts

RuleSpec
Length15 to 30 seconds
CountUp to 3 app previews per localization, shown before your screenshots
iPhone size886 × 1920 portrait, 1920 × 886 landscape
iPad size1200 × 1600 portrait, 1600 × 1200 landscape
Mac and Apple TV1920 × 1080, landscape only
Frame rateUp to 30 fps
CodecH.264 or ProRes 422 HQ
File sizeUp to 500MB
ContentFootage from inside the app, captured on device. No hands, no over the shoulder shots
The rule people trip on. App previews must show only what happens inside the app. A beautiful lifestyle shot of someone using your app at a cafe is a rejection. Save that footage for your socials and keep the preview pure screen capture

The other store

Google Play works differently

Google Play does not take a video upload at all. Your preview video is a YouTube link on the listing, with the 1024 by 500 feature graphic as its cover image

  • Link a single video, not a playlist or channel, set to public or unlisted
  • Ads and monetization must be off or Google Play will not show it
  • Landscape and portrait both work, match your app's orientation
  • Only the first 30 seconds autoplays, muted, so front load the good part
  • At least 80 percent of the video must show the real app experience

You can still design here. Export your composition as MP4, upload it to YouTube, and design the feature graphic in the same project

Questions

Fair questions, straight answers

What size does an App Store app preview need to be?

886 by 1920 for modern iPhones in portrait, 1200 by 1600 for iPads, at up to 30 fps in H.264 or ProRes 422 HQ with a 500MB limit. The store ready conform here targets the iPhone spec automatically

How long can an app preview be?

Between 15 and 30 seconds, up to three per localization. They appear before your screenshots on the product page

Can I show hands using the app?

No. Apple requires previews to show only content from within the app, captured on device. A person holding the phone or fingers tapping the screen gets rejected

Does video export work in every browser?

Video export uses WebCodecs, which Chrome, Edge and the desktop app support. On browsers without it the export stops with a clear message. PNG screenshot export works everywhere

How do Google Play preview videos work?

Google Play does not take a video upload. You link a YouTube video on your listing, set to public or unlisted with ads disabled. Both orientations are supported and only the first 30 seconds autoplays, muted

Is there a watermark?

No. Exports are clean, in every mode, always. There is no paid tier holding features back
