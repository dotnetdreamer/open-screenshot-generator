Google Play screenshot sizes for 2026

Google is looser than Apple. Screenshots just need each side between 320 and 3840 pixels with no side more than twice the other. The numbers that actually matter are 1080 by 1920 for featuring eligibility, 1024 by 500 for the feature graphic, and 512 by 512 for the icon

Checked against Google's published preview asset requirements on July 18, 2026

The short version

Every asset on a Play listing

AssetSize in pixelsStatusRules
Phone screenshots 320 to 3840 per side Required At least 2 across device types to publish, up to 8 per device type, JPEG or 24 bit PNG, no alpha, longer side at most twice the shorter
Feature graphic 1024 × 500 Required JPEG or 24 bit PNG, no transparency, doubles as the preview video cover
App icon 512 × 512 Required 32 bit PNG with alpha, up to 1024KB, no ranking or price badges
Tablet and Chromebook 1080 to 7680 per side Recommended 16:9 landscape or 9:16 portrait, at least 4 to show the large screen experience
Wear OS 384 × 384 minimum For Wear apps Square 1:1, no transparent backgrounds or masking
Android TV banner 1280 × 720 For TV apps At least one TV screenshot plus the TV banner to publish
The featuring rule. To be eligible for the large recommendation formats, apps must provide at least four screenshots at 1080px or better, in 16:9 for landscape (1920 by 1080 minimum) or 9:16 for portrait (1080 by 1920 minimum). Games need at least three. This is the single best reason to export at 1080 by 1920 instead of something smaller

Video

The preview video works differently

Google Play does not take a video upload. The preview video is a YouTube URL on your listing, shown before your screenshots with the feature graphic as its cover

  • Use a single video URL, not a playlist or channel link, with no extra parameters
  • Set the video to public or unlisted, never private, and not age restricted
  • Turn ads and monetization off or the video will not show on Google Play
  • Landscape and portrait are both supported, match your app's orientation
  • Only the first 30 seconds autoplays, muted, so front load the good part
  • At least 80 percent of the video must show the real app experience

Questions

Fair questions, straight answers

What size should Google Play screenshots be in 2026?

Each side between 320 and 3840 pixels, longer side at most twice the shorter. In practice 1080 by 1920 covers phones and also satisfies the 1080px minimum Google requires before featuring your app

How many screenshots does Google Play require?

A minimum of two across device types to publish, up to 8 per device type. For featuring, apps need at least four at 1080px or better in 16:9 or 9:16, games at least three

Is the feature graphic required?

Yes. Every listing must include a 1024 by 500 feature graphic in JPEG or 24 bit PNG with no transparency. It doubles as the cover for your preview video. The Play Store screenshot generator ships a preset at exactly that size

Can screenshots have transparency?

No. Screenshots and the feature graphic must be JPEG or 24 bit PNG without alpha. Only the 512 by 512 app icon uses a 32 bit PNG with alpha

Does Google still have separate 7 inch and 10 inch tablet specs?

Current guidance folds tablets in with Chromebooks under the large screens rules: 1080 to 7680 pixels in 16:9 or 9:16, with at least four screenshots recommended
